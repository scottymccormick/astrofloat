Astrofloat

How to Play

Goal

You are an astronaut and your ship exploded while you were on a spacewalk! Survive the perils of space until your ship can reach you. Don't get hit by asteroids and make sure to replenish your oxygen. Luckily your ship was full of supplies but you have to reach the supplies however you can.

Controls

Use the directional keys in order to move around the screen.

Components

Getting hit by an asteroid lowers your health. The longer you are in contact, the lower your health falls.

Your air steadily leak over time. Pick up an oxygen tank in order to increase your oxygen levels.

If your health goes to zero, it's game over! To regain health, grab a medic pack.
